Timi Sangai

Yadi jeewan baaki xa mero vane timi sangai bitwos sabai
Harek bihaani timro muskaan bata suru hos, raat timrai yaad ma samai

Timro haat samayera hidna pau, jaha bhagya le lanxa
Dukha pani sajilo lagxa, jaba timro saath le sath dinxa

Huri aaye pani daraudina, timi chau vane sath ma
Samaya rokna sakdina, tara maya basxa yo haath ma

Sadhai ko lagi hoina vane pani, jati xa teti timrai naam
Yadi jeewan baaki xa mero vane, timi nai mero sabai pehchaan


Jindagi ko harek mod ma, timi nai rahos saathi
Chupchap basda pani bujhne, timi kati baathi

Thakera jaba ma harauxu, timro aankha banos ghara
Bina sabda pani bhannu sakne, timi nai mero bhara

Samaya le pariksha liyo vane, maya kam nahos kahile
Tuteka sapana pani jodne, timro biswas le aile

Antim saans samma yadi saath chhutena vane Jeewan ko artha etimai xa
Ma timro  timi mero yeti mai mero sansar purna xa
