Pahilo Nazar

Jaba pahilo choti dekhey unlai
Samaya adkiyeko jasto lagyo
Bhid ustai thiyo tara
Mero sansar aachanak sano vayo jasto lagyo


Unle malai nahere pani
Mero maan le unlai chini sakeko thiyo
Jastai aauta adhuro katha
Aauta wakya mai pura vaye jasto thiyo


Uni sanga bolna aat dhilo aayo
Bolna jada dhadkan xito vayo
Unko aakha bolirahe
Mero maan le sabai vanidiyo


Tyo xyan ma, vawisya le dhoka kholena
Tara ni maan le biswas garyo
Yo vet kunai samyog haina
Daiba ko kunai khela thiyo

Kura aaghi badhdai gayo
Mero hridaya maanmagna vayo
Hridaya ko atut biswas le hoki
Maya le naam payo

Pahilo dristi ko maya 
Safal hunxa vani duniya lai dekhaunu xa
Mero hridaya ma vayeko kampan 
Sara biswo lai sunaunu xa


Yadi bato kathin vaye pani 
Ti komal haat xodne xaina
Sangai kataunu xa jeewan
