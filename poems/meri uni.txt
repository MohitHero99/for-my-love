Meri uni

yo mutu dhadkinxa matra timro naam liyera
aauxa jhajhalko aakha ma sansar ko sabai sundar drisya sajhiyera
raati ko mitho sapana bani aauxau timi 
bihani ni aauxau mero sansar ma ujyalo chariyera


aaba timi bina ta jeewan ko yatra ni adhuro lagxa
yo mutu le aaba jahile timro sath magxa						
dhadkan ko taal ma lukeko usko sangeet le ni
usko naram boli ko raag magxa


timro nazar ma samaya nai thamiyeko jasto
timro aak sparsa le sabai dukha haraune jasto
timi sanga rahada harek pal amulya lagxa
timro sath bina mero sansar sunsan ra aapurna jasto lagxa


sapana ko bagaicha ma timi sundar phool jhai fulchyau
mero maan ko kabita bhitra timi mitho laya bani gunjinxau
yo dhadkan ko saas sabai timi lai samarpit garey
mero harek swas ma timi nai basirahanxau
